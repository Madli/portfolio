Enabling MicroLED Displays: Mass-Transfer Technology Brief

AJ Jain, CTO of VerLASE Technologies LLC will expose a new Mass Transfer technology the company has been developing. The technology is based on a novel Photo-Mechanical- Actuation principle, wherein a novel transfer mask architecture along with proprietary chemistry is employed to deterministically manipulate and move large numbers of very small micro die quickly. The mask architecture addresses critical pain points experienced by general Mass Transfer methods, including: individual die addressability for repair / replacement, allowance of native R,G,B display architecture. Key components are being developed with existing hardware infrastructure in mind so as to minimize development time and cost
